QUANTITATIVE RATING

Certainty Coordination Index

The platform computes an automated metric (CCI-0 through CCI-3) for every upcoming dispatch. This index determines the depth of operational coordination required.

CCI-0 (BASE)

Standard private charters utilizing routine domestic routings with simple parameters. Automated check-in tracking.

CCI-1 (ELEVATED)

Multi-leg regional missions requiring custom routing clearances or specialized ground handling agreements.

CCI-2 (CRITICAL)

Complex international operations, VIP protection requirements, or strict medical positioning parameters.

CCI-3 (MAXIMUM CERTAINTY)

Maximum security, diplomatic, or critical emergency response operations. Full human-telemetry integration.

HOLE #1: THE COST OF UNCERTAINTY

Why Certainty Matters

Every operator has paid the uncertainty tax. The uncertainty tax looks different every week:

Individually these problems feel small. Collectively they cost operators millions of Naira, thousands of staff hours, and countless unnecessary headaches.

The Architecture of Certainty exists to reduce the uncertainty tax.

The objective isn't to find fault. The objective is to protect the schedule. That is pure doctrine.

SYSTEM CLARIFICATIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you really wait for absolute escrow clearance before letting my team plan a route?

Yes. We protect your dispatch crew's schedule like our own. Planning fuel releases or filing international airspace routes based on screenshots is banned on our architecture. The funds must be fully cleared in escrow.

How long does a client have to fulfill Tier 2 escrow funds after booking request?

Each mission possesses a customized countdown gate based on the departure timing threshold. If the client fails to clear their holding escrow prior to gate closure, the request is systematically canceled.

What if my aircraft has a mechanical issue? Am I penalized?

Aviation is reality-driven. Mechanical issues occur. If a fault is logged immediately, cooperative re-routing activates without structural system penalty. Delaying notifications or attempting verbal workarounds is what drops your readiness score.

Why are you so strict about written timestamp declarations?

Because "He said, she said" has never satisfied a civil aviation authority or secured a multi-million Naira fuel release. Clear written documentation keeps the system fast and legal departments silent.
